NEASW Welfare Foundation Supports Environmental Awareness Initiative

On 3 June 2026, NEASW Welfare Foundation supported a drawing and painting competition at Government Upper Primary School (GUPS), Kamhua Noknu, themed "Protect Earth, Conserve Nature." Over 300 students participated, promoting environmental awareness through creative expression.

Class-wise prizes and refreshments were distributed to all participants. The initiative was coordinated by NEASW Director Kenny Riba in collaboration with the school, with the support of the Headmaster, teachers, and students.
