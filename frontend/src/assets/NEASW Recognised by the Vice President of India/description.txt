North East Association for Social Welfare (NEASW) participated in the National Tribal Youth Conclave 2026 held from 12th to 13th April at Bharat Mandapam, Delhi, on the inspiring theme “Transforming Tribal Lives through Science and Technology.”

We were honoured to receive recognition for our continued contribution towards the welfare, empowerment, and upliftment of the people of Northeast India, presented by the *Vice President of India, Shri C.P. Radhakrishnan.*

Adding to this proud milestone, our *Chairman Shri Nyaken Riba* was specially invited as an Honoured Speaker, Guest of Honour, and Youth Coordinator for the conclave, an acknowledgement of NEASW’s dedication to creating meaningful change and representing the voices of the Northeast at the national stage.

This recognition motivates us to continue serving our communities with greater passion, unity, and purpose. The journey continues.
